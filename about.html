About Milan Crochet

Milan Crochet was born from a passion for preserving traditional craftsmanship while creating beautiful, functional items for modern homes. Based in the heart of Ngong Town, Kenya, we are a collective of skilled artisans who have been perfecting the art of crochet for generations.

Our journey began in 2020 when our founder, Milan, decided to turn her grandmother's teachings into a sustainable business that could support local artisans while bringing handmade beauty into homes across Kenya and beyond.

Every stitch tells a story of dedication, creativity, and love. We believe that handmade items carry a special energy that mass-produced goods simply cannot match. When you choose Milan Crochet, you're not just buying a product – you're supporting local artisans and becoming part of our extended family.
